Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Who We Are

KiddBusy ("we," "us," or "our") operates KiddBusy.com, a platform that helps parents find kid-friendly activities, events, and businesses in their city.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email addresses when you sign up for our newsletter or submit a review
  • Business information when you submit a listing or sponsor inquiry
  • Review content including your name, rating, written review, and your child's age range
  • Billing information when you purchase a sponsored placement (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card numbers)

We also automatically collect standard web data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages through analytics tools (Google Analytics or Plausible).

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our weekly KiddBusy newsletter (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To process and display business listings and reviews
  • To contact sponsor leads about advertising opportunities
  • To improve the app and understand which cities and listings are most useful
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies

We use cookies to remember your city preference and to understand how parents use our site. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to ad networks. You can decline non-essential cookies using the banner at the bottom of our site.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database provider (data stored in the US)
  • Stripe — payment processing for sponsor subscriptions
  • Resend — email delivery for newsletters and notifications
  • Anthropic — AI processing of submitted content for review and moderation (no personal data is used to train AI models)

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. California residents have additional rights under CCPA. To make a request, email privacy [at] kiddbusy.com.

Children's Privacy

KiddBusy is a platform for parents — we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Reviews ask for a child's age range only to help other parents, not to identify any individual child.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy [at] kiddbusy.com.
